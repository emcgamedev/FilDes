Buko Pandan

A creamy and refreshing dessert with layers of pandan-flavored gelatin, young coconut (buko) strips, jackfruit (langka), and sago pearls.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

0 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

In a pot, combine water, pandan leaves, and agar-agar powder. Let it sit for 10 minutes.

step 2

Bring the mixture to a boil, then add sugar and food coloring (if using). Stir until dissolved.

step 3

Strain the mixture into a mold or baking dish and refrigerate for at least 3 hours to set.

step 4

Once the gelatin is set, arrange the young coconut strips, jackfruit strips, and cooked sago pearls on top.

step 5

Pour the evaporated milk and cream (if using) over the gelatin and fruit mixture.

step 6

Refrigerate for another hour before serving.

Ingredients

Saba bananas (4-6 pcs, ripe)

Coconut milk (2 cups)

Brown sugar (3/4 cup)

Water (1 1/2 cups)

Shaved ice (1 1/2 cups)

Evaporated milk (1/4 cup)

Vanilla extract (optional, 1 tsp)

Tools

Pot

Strainer

Spoon

Mold

Knife
