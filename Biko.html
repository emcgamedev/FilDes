Biko (Sticky Rice with Coconut Milk and Brown Sugar)

A rich and chewy dessert made with glutinous rice cooked in coconut milk and brown sugar.

prep time

40 min.

cook time

0 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Wash glutinous rice and soak for at least 4 hours.

step 2

In a pot, combine rice, coconut milk, brown sugar, and pandan leaves (if using).

step 3

Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the rice becomes sticky and the liquid is absorbed.

step 4

Transfer to a mold lined with banana leaves (optional).

step 5

Steam for 30 minutes.

step 6

Garnish with sesame seeds and let cool before slicing.

ingredients

Glutinous rice (2 cups)

Coconut milk (2 cups)

Brown sugar (1 cup)

Pandan leaves (2 pcs, optional)

Sesame seeds (for garnish, optional)

tools

Pot

Steamer

Mold

Knife
