Mais Con Yelo

A refreshing and light dessert perfect for hot days. It combines cooked sweet corn kernels with sweetened condensed milk, cream, and shaved ice.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

0 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Cook the sweet corn: In a pot, boil water with sugar. Add sweet corn kernels and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until tender. Drain and let cool slightly.

step 2

Combine the wet ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together condensed milk, cream, and vanilla extract (if using).

step 3

Assemble: In a serving bowl or tall glass, layer shaved ice with cooked sweet corn kernels. Pour the creamy mixture over the top.

step 4

Enjoy immediately!

Ingredients

Sweet corn kernels (1 cup, fresh or frozen)

Water (1 cup)

Sugar (1/4 cup)

Condensed milk (1/2 cup)

Cream (1/2 cup)

Shaved ice (around 2 cups)

Vanilla extract (optional, 1 tsp)

Tools

Pot

spoon

whisk

serving bowl

tall glass
