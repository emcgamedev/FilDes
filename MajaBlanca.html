Maja Blanca

A creamy and smooth coconut pudding with a hint of corn and a sweet cheese or latik topping.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

0 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together cornstarch and 1/2 cup of water to form a slurry.

step 2

Combine wet ingredients: In a pot, combine coconut milk, remaining water, sugar, and evaporated milk (if using). Heat over medium heat, stirring constantly until the sugar dissolves.

step 3

Thicken the mixture: Slowly pour the cornstarch slurry into the hot mixture while whisking continuously to avoid lumps. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until thickened like pudding.

step 4

Pour and cool: Pour the mixture into a mold or individual serving dishes. Let it cool completely at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours to set.

step 5

Top and serve: Before serving, top with grated cheese or latik.

Ingredients

Coconut milk (2 cups)

Cornstarch (1/2 cup)

Water (2 cups)

Sugar (1/2 cup, adjust to taste)

Evaporated milk (optional, 1/2 cup)

Cream cheese (grated, for topping) or Latik (caramelized coconut milk, for topping)

Tools

Pot

spoon

whisk

measuring cup

grater (for cheese topping)
