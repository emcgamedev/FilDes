Maruya

Deep-fried banana fritters with a crispy golden brown exterior and a soft, sweet banana filling.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

0 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Mash the bananas: In a large bowl, mash the ripe bananas using a fork or potato masher.

step 2

Make the batter: Add flour, brown sugar, and baking powder to the mashed bananas. Mix well.

step 3

Adjust consistency: Gradually add milk while whisking to create a thick but pourable batter. If needed, add a little water to achieve the right consistency.

step 4

Frying: Heat oil in a deep fryer or pan to medium-high heat (around 350Â°F).

step 5

Scooping and frying: Scoop the batter by tablespoons and carefully drop it into the hot oil. Fry for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy.

step 6

Drain and serve: Drain the fried maruya on paper towels to remove excess oil. Serve hot and enjoy!

Ingredients

Ripe saba bananas (around 4 pcs)

All-purpose flour (1 cup)

Brown sugar (1/4 cup, or to taste)

Baking powder (1 tsp)

Milk (1/2 cup)

Water (optional, for adjusting batter consistency)

Cooking oil for frying

Tools

Large Bowl

fork or potato masher

whisk

spoon

deep fryer or pan

paper towel
