Turon

A deep-fried spring roll filled with caramelized saba bananas (plantains) and wrapped in a thin flour wrapper (lumpia wrapper). Often drizzled with honey or jackfruit syrup for extra sweetness.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

0 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Caramelize the sugar: In a pan, melt brown sugar with water over medium heat, stirring constantly until it becomes a golden brown syrup. Set aside.

step 2

Prepare the bananas: Peel the saba bananas and cut them lengthwise into thin slices.

step 3

Assemble the turon: Lay a lumpia wrapper flat on a work surface. Place a few banana slices in a diagonal line near the edge of the wrapper. Drizzle a little caramelized sugar syrup on top of the bananas. Fold the wrapper over the bananas, then fold the sides inwards to form a tight roll. Seal the edges with a dab of water to prevent them from unrolling while frying. Repeat with the remaining bananas and wrappers.

step 4

Frying: Heat oil in a deep fryer or pan to medium-high heat (around 350Â°F). Carefully lower the turon rolls into the hot oil and fry for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy.

step 5

Drain and serve: Drain the fried turon on paper towels to remove excess oil. Serve hot and enjoy!

Ingredients

Saba bananas (around 4 pcs, slightly ripe)

Lumpia wrappers (around 10 pcs)

Brown sugar (1/2 cup)

Water (1/4 cup)

Cooking oil for frying

Tools

Plate

paper towel

tong

deep fryer or pan

knife
