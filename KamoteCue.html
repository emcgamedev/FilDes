Kamote Cue

Deep-fried sweet potato slices coated in caramelized brown sugar.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

0 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Wash and peel the sweet potatoes. Cut them into thin slices (around 1/4 inch thick).

step 2

If using skewers, pierce each slice through the center.

step 3

Heat oil in a pan for frying.

step 4

In a separate pan, melt brown sugar with water to create a caramelized coating.

step 5

While hot, dip the fried sweet potatoes in the caramelized brown sugar coating.

step 6

Let them cool slightly before serving.

Ingredients

Sweet potatoes (2-3 pcs, medium)

Brown sugar (1/2 cup)

Water (1/4 cup)

Cooking oil for frying

Skewers (optional)

Tools

Deep fryer or pan

skewer (optional)

tongs

Knife
